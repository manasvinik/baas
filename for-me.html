Is Boss as a Service for me?

Great question.

Boss as a Service isn't for everyone, so what we'll do here is be straightforward about the way it works, so you can make a call.

About us. We're here to demand answers when you're slacking, so we'll be tough and sometimes even annoyingly relentless. You should expect that, and indeed, want that from us, for this to work.

About you. People who have best results with Boss as a Service see themselves in most of the following:

  • You're unhappy with your current productivity rate, and you know you can accomplish much more. You're serious about committing to productivity and accomplishing everything you planned to.
  • You're self-aware about your patterns of productivity hitherto. You know you have issues with akrasia and procrastination, and actively want to fix them.
  • You've tried just about every todo app out there, but they don't stick. You realize that what you need is not a spiffy new UI. The problem occurs not because you don't have a cool way of writing down todos, but because you do not follow through on what you planned.
  • You've observed that you work far better as part of a team or when you have someone to supervise you, because in those situations, you are forced to follow through.
  • You sometimes self-sabotage your own productivity systems, which leads to their collapse. (Don't worry, we've all done this. This is also why we might need some external help and judgement.)
