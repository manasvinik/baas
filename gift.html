How it works

On the day you select, the recipient will automatically be sent an email with your message and a link to activate their subscription (they can choose a different email address at that time, too, if they like). If the recipient is already a member of Boss as a Service, the gift will be added on to the end of their current subscription.

Just one thing to note: usually, when you're purchasing a membership for yourself, even if you don't spring into action at once, we do! If you don't start working / sending us your goals, we'll start to proactively remind you to do so. Hey, that's what we do -- and that's what you signed up for! But for gift subscriptions, we don't do this until the person conveys their willingness to be bugged by us, by activating the subscription. We don't want to bug people who don't want to be bugged!

Will I be charged again in the future?

No. When the subscription expires the gift recipient will have the opportunity to renew with their own credit card (or, you can give another gift). But it won't be automatically renewed using your payment information.

What if the recipient already has a Boss as a Service subscription?

If the recipient already has a BAAS subscription, then the gift will extend that subscription. For example, if their current subscription is set to expire on November 1, 2020, a one-year gift will extend their subscription to November 1, 2021.

I'm not sure about what email address the recipient will want to use

No worries. When the recipient receives their gift they will be able to create a new account with any email address they choose (or use an account they may have previously created on Boss as a Service). In case you need to change email addresses, contact us using our live chat and we'll help you out!
