Vishwaroopa PTE uses the following data processors with respect to the personal information collected:

We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 18 or knowingly allow such persons to register with the Services. Privacy Rights

You have the following rights on this website:

Right to be forgotten: You may cancel your account at any time. If you wish your data to be permanently deleted, you may contact us at admin@Bossasaservice.life and it will be deleted within 72 hours.

Right to object: We may use anonymized data for the purposes of improving our service. In the event you would prefer we do not use your data, you may opt out from any such project by emailing us at admin@Bossasaservice.life with the subject line “Opt Out of Data Science”.

Right to access and rectification: You can contact us at admin@Bossasaservice.life at any time to access copies of all user data we retain for you. We will remove, update, or change any personal information about you immediately upon your request.

Right of portability: We will provide information to you upon your request.

Right of restriction: You can make a request for restriction or suppression of your personal data.

In addition to the above, an “unsubscribe” option is automatically included in the footer of every newsletter sent from Bossasaservice.life. This allows you to easily unsubscribe from the newsletter. In the event you unsubscribe, we will stop sending you the newsletter. Please note that unsubscribing from the newsletter with the newsletter’s “unsubscribe” button does not cancel your account with Vishwaroopa PTE. In order to do so, please log in at Bossasaservice.life and cancel your account.
